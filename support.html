Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create an account?
Click on "Create New Account" on the home page, enter your details, and verify your email or phone number.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.
How do I contact a seller?
Click on any listing, then use the "Message" button to chat directly with the seller through our secure messaging system.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT) through our secure Wallet system.
How do I report a problem?
Use the "Report" button on any listing or contact our support team via email, phone, or live chat for immediate assistance.
